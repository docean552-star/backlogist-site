Happy path: 6 variants diverge at PLANNING (marketing→BRIEFING) and IN_PROGRESS (code→IN_REVIEW, seo→DATA_REVIEW, marketing→REVIEW, think→direct AA).
Fallbacks: every review stage can reject back to IN_PROGRESS. Operator can reject from AWAITING_APPROVAL. BLOCKED from any active state, recovers when blocker resolved. DONE auto-reopens when child created.
Terminal: CANCELLED, SUPERSEDED, WONT-DO reachable from anywhere. TODO is legacy alias.
